How it works

Three steps to a
focused classroom.

1
Teacher creates a session
Open AD-HD, name your class, and get a shareable 6-digit code in seconds. No signup required to start.
2
Students join instantly
Students enter the code on any device. They land in a shared live note editor alongside their peers.
3
Operator sends nudges
Push summaries, clarity checkpoints, and gentle audio cues to keep every student anchored — without disrupting the flow.
Features

Everything a distracted mind
actually needs.

📝
Live peer-driven notes
All students write in a single shared document. Contributions appear instantly with peer attribution — no one falls behind alone.
✓
Active clarity checkpoints
The teacher pushes micro-questions mid-lecture. Students respond in one tap. The operator sees who's lost — before they zone out entirely.
Real-time feedback
△
Manual engagement nudges
Reorient, breathe, stretch, or quick-check nudges are sent by the instructor at any moment — with a gentle musical tone, not a jarring alert.
◎
Real audio, not noise
Each nudge type plays a distinct, carefully tuned sine-wave tone — rising chime, arpeggio, low swell. Designed to redirect, not startle.
ADHD-considered
◷
Post-session efficacy survey
Every session ends with a 3-question check-in. Did the nudges help without distracting you? How focused did you feel? We track the core assumption: can an interactive tool sustain attention without becoming a distraction itself?
